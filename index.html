Feedback And Security

Use public issues for product feedback that can be discussed openly. Keep private account, billing, uploaded media, logs, and vulnerability details out of public tickets.

For private support or security-sensitive reports, contact support@productx.video. Production source code, provider credentials, billing configuration, and deployment secrets are not published in the public repository.
